## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Nexus Corporation delivered strong Q4 2024 results, exceeding analyst expectations across all key metrics. Revenue grew 23% year-over-year to $892 million, driven by robust demand in our Cloud Services and Enterprise Solutions segments. Operating margin expanded to 18.5%, reflecting successful cost optimization initiatives.

### Key Highlights
- **Revenue:** $892M (+23% YoY, +8% QoQ)
- **Gross Profit:** $534M (59.9% margin)
- **Operating Income:** $165M (18.5% margin)
- **Net Income:** $127M ($2.54 per diluted share)
- **Free Cash Flow:** $142M

## 2. SEGMENT PERFORMANCE

### 2.1 Cloud Services ($412M)

Our Cloud Services segment achieved record revenue of $412 million, representing 38.3% year-over-year growth.

**Key Drivers:**
- Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) reached $1.52 billion (+41% YoY)
- Net Revenue Retention: 118%
- 127 new enterprise customers added (>$100K ARR)
- Average contract value increased to $285K (+22%)

**Product Performance:**
| Product | Q4 Revenue | Growth |
|---------|------------|--------|
| NexusCloud Core | $198M | +42% |
| NexusData Analytics | $124M | +35% |
| NexusAI Platform | $90M | +48% |

### 2.2 Enterprise Solutions ($287M)

Enterprise Solutions delivered $287 million in revenue with healthy 14.3% growth.

**Highlights:**
- Won 3 Fortune 100 accounts worth $45M total contract value
- Completed integration of Pinnacle Software acquisition
- Government sector grew 28% to $52M

**Major Contract Wins:**
- GlobalBank Corp: $18M (5-year deal)
- United Healthcare Systems: $15M (3-year deal)
- Federal Agency (classified): $12M

### 2.3 Professional Services ($118M)

Professional Services revenue of $118 million reflects our focus on customer success.

- Implementation services: $72M
- Training & certification: $28M
- Consulting: $18M
- Utilization rate: 78% (target: 75%)

### 2.4 Licensing & Support ($75M)

Legacy licensing revenue remained stable at $75 million.

- Maintenance renewals: 92% rate
- Transitioning customers to subscription model
- Expected decline of 5-10% annually as migrations continue
